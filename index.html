About

I am a researcher in the Jaffe Group at the Harvard Department of Physics, where I am interested in quantum complexity and topics in quantum information, more broadly. I recently graduated from Harvard, where I studied Physics and Mathematics. During Summer 2024, I was an Quantum Error Correction REU researcher at the University of Maryland, College Park advised by Prof. Victor V. Albert and Prof. Nathanan Tantivasadakarn. Prior to that, I was a Quantum Computing SULI Intern at Brookhaven National Laboratory under the mentorship of Prof. Layla Hormozi.
